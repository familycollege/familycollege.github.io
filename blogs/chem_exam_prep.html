Chemistry Olympiad Info

Prepare the exam

Past exams can be viewed at Chemistry Olympiad Past Exams. Other various subjects can be viewed at ACS tutorials website.

Questions on the Local exam and Part 1 of the National exam are arranged by subject and the answers to the exams are posted on the website:

Questions 1-6: Stoichiometry/Solutions

Questions 7-12: Descriptive/Laboratory

Questions 13-18: States of Matter

Questions 19-24: Thermodynamics

Questions 25-30: Kinetics

Questions 31-36: Equilibrium

Questions 37-42: Oxidation-Reduction

Questions 43-48: Atomic Structure/Periodicity

Questions 49-54: Bonding/Molecular Structure

Questions 55-60: Organic/Biochemistry
